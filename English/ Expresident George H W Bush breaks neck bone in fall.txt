<p>Former United States President George H W Bush has fallen at home in the US state of Maine and broken a bone in his neck.</p>
<p>Spokesman Jim McGrath said that the 91-year-old Bush was in stable condition and is doing &#x201C;fine&#x201D; after Wednesday&#x2019;s fall.</p>
<p>He said in a tweet that the 41st president will be in a neck brace.</p>

<p>Bush was hospitalised in Houston in December for about a week for treatment of shortness of breath. He said he was &#x201C;grateful to the doctors and nurses for their superb care&#x201D; after his treatment there.</p>
<p>Bush is the oldest living former US president. He served two terms as Ronald Reagan&#x2019;s vice president before being elected the nation&#x2019;s president in 1988.</p>

<p>After one term highlighted by the success of the 1991 Gulf War in Kuwait he lost to Democrat Bill Clinton amid voters&#x2019; concerns about the economy.</p>
<p>The father of fellow ex-president and namesake George W Bush and <a href="http://www.aljazeera.com/news/2015/06/jeb-bush-confirms-enter-presidential-race-150604111906564.html">2016 presidential contender Jeb Bush</a> spent two months in hospital in 2012 and 2013, including a stretch in intensive care, for a bronchitis-related cough and associated fever.</p>
<p>Bush also suffers from Parkinson&#x2019;s disease, which has confined him to a motorised wheelchair in recent years.</p>

<p>George H W Bush, the former US president, is being treated treated for bronchitis in hospital in Houston, Texas,&#xA0;medical officials say.</p>
<p>The <em>Houston Chronicle</em> reported on&#xA0;Thursday that Bush, 88, has a chronic cough and that his condition is not life-threatening.</p>
<p>He has been in the hospital for a week and is expected to be released this weekend, the newspaper reported.</p>
<p>Bush, a Republican and the 41st president, took office in 1989 and lost his re-election bid in 1992 to Democrat Bill<br>Clinton.</p>
<p>The father of former President George W Bush, he served as a congressman, UN ambassador, envoy to China, CIA director and two-term vice president under Ronald Reagan.</p>
<p>As president, Bush routed Iraq after Saddam Hussein had invaded Kuwait in 1990,&#xA0;with his his approval ratings soaring to the 90 per cent range.</p>
<p>But just 20 months later he was be defeated&#xA0;by Clinton. Bush is the oldest living former president and a World War II veteran.</p>

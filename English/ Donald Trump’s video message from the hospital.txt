<p>US President Donald Trump has released a video message on Twitter saying his health condition is &#x201C;much better&#x201D;, before adding that the next days &#x201C;will be a test&#x201D;.</p>
<p>Trump spoke on Saturday from his room at the Walter Reed National Military Medical Center where he was admitted on Friday for coronavirus treatment.</p>
<p>&#x201C;I came here I wasn&#x2019;t feeling so well. I feel much better now,&#x201D; the president said in the video. &#x201C;We are working hard to get me all the way back, I have to be back because we still have to make America great again,&#x201D; he added.</p>
